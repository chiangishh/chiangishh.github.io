Miss Taiwanese American 👑

For over two years, I’ve volunteered with a nonprofit program as a Design and Marketing lead under the Taiwanese American Citizens League, dedicated to empowering Taiwanese American entrepreneurs. This experience allowed me to support several startups during AAPI Heritage Month through my role as a leader in my company’s API Employee Network. I’ve really valued the opportunity to help bridge the gap between Taiwan and America—something I personally relate to, having grown up in a community where I often had to build that bridge for myself.
