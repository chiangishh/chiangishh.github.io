We learned that AAPI supporters feel strongly about recent hate crimes against the community and want to spread awareness and decrease crime.

Young supporters feel enraged for the AAPI community, while the older demographics are experiencing fear. We as UX Designers would like to help by creating an application designed to promote optimism while spreading awareness of the grave situation.
